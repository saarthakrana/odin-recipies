Rajma



Kheer, also known as payasam or payesh, is a pudding or porridge popular in South Asia, usually made by boiling milk, sugar or jaggery, and rice. It can be additionally flavoured with dried fruits, nuts, cardamom and saffron. Instead of rice, it may contain cracked wheat, vermicelli, sago or tapioca.
Ingredients:
  • Milk
  • Rice
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Dry fruits
